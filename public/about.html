Our Story

Built for Real People,
Right Here in Northern Tel Aviv

Personal training that meets you where you are

Pushkash Fitness House was born from a simple belief: that getting fit should feel encouraging, not overwhelming. After years of coaching people across northern Tel Aviv, I saw how many talented, motivated individuals were held back — not by lack of will, but by generic programs that never truly fit their lives. So I built something different. A training space rooted in honest conversation, realistic goal-setting, and the kind of personal attention that actually moves the needle.

Whether you're returning to movement after a long pause, looking to build strength with purpose, or simply want someone in your corner who genuinely understands your schedule and your body — Pushkash Fitness House is here for you. Every session is designed around you, and every milestone we reach together is one that lasts.

Our Coaching Philosophy

Training That WorksWithYou

At Pushkash Fitness House, we believe the best results come not from pushing harder, but from moving smarter — with intention, patience, and a genuine understanding of where you are right now. Our approach is built around supporting people, not burning them out.

01

Train with Intention

Every session has a purpose. We don't just go through the motions — each movement, each set, and each rest period is designed with your specific goals in mind. Mindful training means you leave every workout knowing exactly why you did what you did, and feeling better for it.

02

Progress Sustainably

Quick fixes fade. Real transformation takes time, and that's something to embrace, not resist. We build programs that fit your life in northern Tel Aviv — your schedule, your energy levels, your recovery — so that progress compounds naturally over weeks and months, not just days.

03

Build Confidence Through Consistency

Confidence in your body doesn't come from one great workout — it comes from showing up, week after week, and watching yourself get stronger. We celebrate every small win because those are the moments that build the unshakeable foundation of long-term wellbeing.
