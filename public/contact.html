Got Questions?

Everything You Need to Know

Here are the most common questions from people starting their personal training journey in northern Tel Aviv.

Q

Who is this coaching for?

Pushkash Fitness House is designed for anyone in northern Tel Aviv who wants a personalised approach to movement and wellness — whether you're a complete beginner, returning after a break, or an active person looking to train smarter. There's no required fitness level. Sessions are built around you, your goals, and your lifestyle.

Q

Where do sessions take place?

Sessions can take place wherever works best for you. Options include outdoor locations across northern Tel Aviv — parks, the promenade, open spaces — as well as in-home training if you have the space, or at a local gym. The goal is to remove friction and make showing up as easy as possible.

Q

What should I bring to a session?

Just yourself and comfortable workout clothes. For outdoor sessions, bring a water bottle and suitable footwear. Any equipment needed — resistance bands, mats, or small weights — is provided. If you train at a gym, a membership or day pass is required, but nothing else is needed on your end.

Q

How personalised is the programme?

Completely. Every client starts with a one-on-one intake conversation to understand their goals, physical history, schedule, and preferences. From there, a bespoke training plan is built and continuously adjusted as you progress. No two clients follow the same programme — because no two people are the same.
