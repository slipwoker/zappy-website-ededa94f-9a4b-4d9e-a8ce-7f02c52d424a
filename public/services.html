What We Offer

Coaching That Fits Your Life

Every programme at Pushkash Fitness House is shaped around you — your schedule, your starting point, and the kind of progress that actually lasts.

Personal Training for Beginners

Designed for those taking their first real steps into fitness, this programme builds confidence alongside capability. We start with movement fundamentals, work at a pace that suits you, and create habits that stick — no intimidation, no rushing.

Ideal for: adults new to structured training seeking a safe, encouraging start

Strength & Conditioning

A progressive, results-focused programme for those ready to build real strength, improve athletic performance, and reshape their body composition. Structured periodisation, smart recovery, and consistent challenge — all personalised to your goals.

Ideal for: intermediate to advanced clients looking to level up their performance

Mobility & Posture Support

Targeted work for those dealing with stiffness, desk-related tension, or postural imbalances. This programme combines corrective exercise, joint mobility flows, and mindful movement to restore ease and reduce discomfort in daily life.

Ideal for: professionals, desk workers, and anyone recovering from chronic tightness

Outdoor Coaching Sessions

Take your training into the open air of northern Tel Aviv's parks and promenades. Outdoor sessions blend functional fitness, cardio, and bodyweight work in a refreshing environment — energising, varied, and perfectly suited to the local lifestyle.

Ideal for: those who thrive outside and want training that feels like an adventure
